How It Works

- Person cooks some dishes/beverages/etc. for himself or relatives.

- Rest of foods can’t be used (it will spoil soon, no ability to keep it, event was screwed up, want to cook something else. There is a lot of reasons for that, really).

- In our mobile app person creates a page with description of his dishes, adds a picture, location, price and time when person is available.

- It is listed in our app and becomes public.

- People who interested in getting some foods are able to find what they want by using filters.

- They mark the page of person, who created an offer and get what was noted in the way, which was mentioned in description of dishes.
